How to play

* Pick a card that you want to play

* CPUs will take turn to play their card

* CPUs' card to have match your suit

* Whoever have highest card win the round

* If they can't they have to play Space

* Space overrule all other suit

* Highest Space card wins the round

* At the end, whoever has highest score WIN
